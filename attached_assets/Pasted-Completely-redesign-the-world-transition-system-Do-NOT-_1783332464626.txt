Completely redesign the world transition system.

Do NOT reuse the same transition animation.

Every world must have its own unique cinematic, entrance, voice, visual effects, sound effects, and music.

The gameplay must pause completely during every cinematic. The player, obstacles, physics and score must freeze until the cinematic finishes.

Each world should feel like entering a completely different universe.

Examples:

1. Neon Space
- Stars appear one by one.
- Neon lights activate.
- Camera zooms in.
- Voice: "Welcome... to Neon Space."

2. Volcano Realm
- The ground cracks.
- Lava erupts.
- Smoke fills the screen.
- Camera shakes.
- Voice: "Welcome... to the Volcano Realm."

3. Serpent Temple
- Darkness covers the screen.
- Two giant glowing snake eyes appear.
- The eyes slowly open.
- A massive serpent roar is heard.
- The temple fades into view.
- Voice: "Welcome... to the Serpent Temple."

4. Ice Kingdom
- Everything freezes.
- Ice spreads across the screen.
- Snowstorm begins.
- The ice breaks to reveal the world.

5. Electric City
- Screen glitches.
- Lightning strikes everywhere.
- Neon buildings power on.

6. Ocean Depths
- The camera dives underwater.
- Bubbles rise.
- Whales and fish pass in the background.

7. Enchanted Forest
- Leaves fall.
- Fireflies appear.
- Trees grow from the ground.

8. Desert Kingdom
- Sandstorm covers the screen.
- Ancient ruins emerge from the sand.

9. Haunted Mansion
- Thunder.
- Ghosts fly across the screen.
- The mansion slowly appears.

10. Future Laboratory
- Holograms activate.
- Digital scan effect.
- Robots power on.

11. Sky Kingdom
- Clouds open.
- Sunlight shines.
- Floating islands appear.

12. Crystal Cave
- Crystals grow from the walls.
- Everything begins to glow.

13. Sakura Garden
- Cherry blossom petals fill the screen.
- Wind animation.
- Peaceful music starts.

14. Shadow Dimension
- Reality breaks apart.
- Dark portals appear.
- Purple energy surrounds everything.

15. Final Realm
- The universe collapses.
- Planets explode.
- Black holes consume stars.
- Space itself cracks.
- A deep voice says:
  "You have reached the end..."
  "Prepare to face the Shadow King."

Every cinematic must feel cinematic, surprising and memorable. No two world transitions should look alike.
